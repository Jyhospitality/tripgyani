MAGICAL MALAYSIA

Booknow

  • DAY 01: ARRIVE KUALA LUMPUR

    Arrive Kuala Lumpur airport in the morning and meet our representative who will assist you for your transfer to the hotel. Check in at your hotel. Later proceed for Kuala Lumpur city tour of Malaysia where you will see PETRONAS Twin Tower (photo stop) and entrance, Independence Square, King Palace & Chocolate outlet. You will also be taken to the top of KL tower. Overnight Stay at hotel in Kuala Lumpur.

  • DAY 2: KUALA LUMPUR (SUNWAY LAGOON TOUR - OPTIONAL)

    After breakfast at hotel. Day free for leisure or shopping or you can take the optional tour of Sunway Lagoon resort to enjoy your day at the wet and dry park (7 hours stay at sun way lagoon). Overnight Stay at hotel in Kuala Lumpur.

  • DAY 3: KUALA LUMPUR – GENTING HIGHLAND

    After breakfast at hotel. You will be picked up from your hotel to be transferred to Genting Highland. You will be visiting Batu caves enroute to Genting Highlands for photo stop. You will be set free to use one of the theme park tickets (indoor &outdoor) and enjoy other activities at the resort. Overnight Stay at hotel in Genting Highland.

  • DAY 4: DEPARTURE FROM GENTING HIGHLAND (KUALA LUMPUR)

    After breakfast at hotel. You will be picked from your hotel to be transferred to Kuala Lumpur airport for your flight back home.

MYSTIC MALAYSIA

Booknow

  • DAY 1: ARRIVE KUALA LUMPUR (LANGKAWI)

    On arrival at the Kuala Lumpur International Airport, you will be met by Tripgyani representative and transferred at your hotel. The rest of your day is free at leisure. Stay overnight at the hotel in Kuala Lumpur.

  • DAY 2: KUALA LUMPUR

    After breakfast, proceed for a full day tour of Kuala Lumpur covering Petronas Twin Tower, Chocolate Factory, KL Tower, Batu Caves & Watch Outlet. Also includes Photo Stop at Kings Palace, China Town, National Monument, National Museum, National Mosque, Independences Square OR Anglican Church, Sultan Abdul Samad Building. Stay overnight at the hotel in Kuala Lumpur.

  • DAY 3: KUALA LUMPUR – LANGKAWI

    After breakfast, check out from the hotel and get transferred to the Kuala Lumpur International Airport for your flight to Langkawi. Arrive Langkawi and transfer to hotel for check in, the rest of your day is at leisure. Stay overnight at hotel in Langkawi.

  • DAY 4: LANGKAWI

    After breakfast, proceed for a half day island tour of Langkawi. A fascinating tour that will take you through the urban and rural areas of Langkawi - the Legendary Island. Begin your journey with a visit to the Atma Alam Handicraft Center and then to the legendary Mahsuri Tomb. Then, visit the Eagle Square and the duty-free Kuah Town. Stay overnight at the hotel Langkawi.

  • DAY 5: DEPART KUALA LUMPUR

    After your breakfast in Langkawi, transfer to the Langkawi International Airport for your flight back home.

  • DAY 6: HONG KONG - DAY FREE FOR LEISURE

    Enjoy breakfast at the hotel. Today the day is free at your own leisure so you can laze up in your bed for little longer. Later you can set out for self-exploration of the city. Explore several city attractions and soak in the glitz and glamour. Experience the modern and cosmopolitan lifestyle and barge on some more shopping. After a wonderful relaxed out day on your own in the benevolent city, retire for the day and get in your cozy bed. Stay overnight at hotel in Hong Kong.

  • DAY 7: DEPARTURE FROM HONG KONG

    Enjoy a relaxed morning followed by breakfast at hotel, check out and enjoy the free day. Spend time in your own preferred day and click loads of pictures on your last day at this beautiful place. Later catch your transfer to the airport to board your return flight. We hope that you had a lovely and memorable time with your loved ones and we will be waiting to receive your feedback after you return.

PENANG DELIGHT

Booknow

  • DAY 1: ARRIVE PENANG

    On arrival at the Penang International Airport, you will be met by Tripgyani representative and transferred to your hotel. While the rest of your day is free at leisure, we recommend you take your own shopping tour in Penang. Popular shopping complexes include Gurney Plaza, Prangin Mall, Island Plaza and Midlands. After sunset, the Batu Ferringhi sidewalk transforms into a bazaar, like the night market everything from food, clothing, batik, watches, pens and fashions accessories are sold here. Stay overnight at hotel in Penang.

  • DAY 2: PENANG – HALF DAY ROUND ISLAND TOUR

    After breakfast, proceed for a half day island tour of Penang. Visit the island’s main attractions such as the War Museum, the Snake Temple, the Botanical Garden and the Butterfly Farm, or explore the industrial and commercial production side of Penang. Stay overnight at hotel in Penang.

  • DAY 3: DEPART PENANG – KUALA LUMPUR

    After breakfast, check out of from the hotel and get transferred to the Penang International Airport for your flight to Kuala Lumpur. Arrive Kuala Lumpur and later check in to the hotel. While the rest of your day is at leisure, we recommend shopping in Kuala Lumpur. Malaysia is a premier shopping destination in Southeast Asia. If you are a shopaholic, the best time of the year to visit is during the Malaysia Mega Sale Carnival, around August – September when the entire country offers great discounts. Spend the day at leisure. Stay overnight at hotel in Kuala Lumpur.

  • DAY 4: KUALA LUMPUR – CITY TOUR

    After breakfast, proceed for a half day City Tour of Kuala Lumpur which includes Photo Stop at Kings Palace, China Town, Notional Monument, National Museum, National Mosque, Shopping Stop, Independences Square OR Anglican Church, sultan Abdul Samad building, Twin Tower & Batu Caves. Stay overnight at hotel in Kuala Lumpur.

  • DAY 5: DEPART - KUALA LUMPUR

    After your breakfast in Kuala Lumpur, transfer to the Kuala Lumpur International Airport for your flight back home.
