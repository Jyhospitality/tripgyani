Go Goa Gone

Booknow

  • DAY 1: ARRIVE GOA

    On Arrival at Goa airport/railway station, meet Tripgyani representative, later proceed to Hotel. At the time arrival Check in at the hotel. Spend evening at leisure and overnight stay at hotel in Goa.

  • DAY 2: SOUTH GOA TOUR

    After breakfast proceed for South Goa tour, visit the oldest and most spectacular of all South Goa Beaches, Colva beach. You can make some of the best memories at the clean beaches with soft white sands. Later visit Shri Shanta Durga Temple, which is dedicated to Goddess of peace, Shanteri and Shri Ramanath Temple. Return back to hotel for overnight stay.

  • DAY 3: NORTH GOA TOUR

    After breakfast Visit major attractions of the place including St. Francis church, Dona Paula, Baga Beach / Calangute Beach, Vagator Beach and old fort - Fort Aguada. Experience natural beauty and tranquility at its best and take back some great memories. Evening is awesome when you witness the night life with freedom in each soul around. Stay overnight at the hotel.

  • DAY 4: DEPARTURE

    After breakfast Transfer to Airport or Railway station for return journey to home.

Holy Amritsar

Booknow

  • DAY 01:- AMRITSAR

    Arrive at Amritsar Railway Station. On arrival meet our driver, later drive to Hotel for check in. Evening visit India-Pakistan Attari Border to watch Flag Retreat Ceremony- Attari Wagah, an army Joint Check Post on India-Pakistan border 30 Kms from Amritsar where the daily highlight is the evening "Beating the Retreat" ceremony. Soldiers from both countries march in perfect drill, going through the steps of bringing down their respective national flags. Return to hotel for overnight stay.

  • DAY 2 AMRITSAR

    After breakfast, start your city tour with the blessings of Golden Temple-the most important Sikh shrine also known as Harmandir Sahib and Durbar Sahib. Then visit Jallianwala Bagh- the place that reminds every Indian of the brutal and heartless massacre by British Gen. Dyer.

    VISIT DURGIANA TEMPLE:The temple the complex houses Shree Laxmi Narayan Mandir with a huge pond on its periphery.

    MATA LAL JI DEVI TEMPLE:The replica Temple of Mata Vaishno Devi Temple in Katra , Jammu and Kashmir. Spend rest of the day for leisure for shopping etc. Return to hotel, overnight at Amritsar Hotel.

  • DAY 3:- AMRITSAR

    After breakfast rest of the day at leisure till Transfer to Railway station/ Airport for return journey to home.

Mahableshwar

Booknow

  • DAY 1:- ARRIVE MAHABALESHWAR

    Arrive for Mahabaleshwar, upon arrival transfer to hotel, Spend day for leisure. Stay overnight at Mahabaleshwar.

  • DAY 2:- MAHABALESHWAR & PANCHGANI TOUR

    Enjoy an early breakfast at the hotel later proceed for Panchgani & Mahabaleshwar tour by tourist taxi on sharing basis. Evening return back to hotel and spend time at leisure. Stay overnight at the hotel.

  • DAY 3:- PRATAPGAD TOUR.

    After breakfast spend day for leisure for local shopping. Later proceed for Pratapgad tour. (Pratapgad route being slippery in monsoons, hence it’s not undertaken from June to October). Stay overnight at the hotel.

  • DAY 4:- DEPARTURE

    After breakfast proceed for Departure with sweet memories of Mahabaleshwar.

Pride of Maharashtra

Booknow

  • DAY 1:- ARRIVE MUMBAI – SHIRDI (240 KMS)

    Arrive from Mumbai airport/ railway station. Meet & assist by Tripgyani representative. Transfer by reserved vehicle to Hotel. In evening visit to Shri Sai Baba temple at the guest’s convenient timing. Guest may please note there is no special or paid Darshan. Stay overnight at hotel.

  • DAY 2:- SHIRDI – SHANISINGANAPUR- SHIRDI

    Morning post breakfast proceed to Shanisinganapur(Shani Shingnapur Temple is dedicated to Lord Shaneshwar or Lord Shanidev). By evening return back to Shirdi. Stay overnight at hotel.

  • DAY 3:- SHIRDI – IGATPURI

    Morning post breakfast proceed for Igatpuri. Meet & assist by Tripgyani representative. Transfer to Rainforest Resort. In night enjoy the DJ Night & Karaoke at resort. Stay overnight at resort.

  • DAY 4:- DEPARTURE

    Morning post breakfast checkout from resort and depart for respective destination.
