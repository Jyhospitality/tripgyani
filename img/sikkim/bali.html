BALI HONEYMOON SPECIAL

Booknow

  • DAY 01: ARRIVE IN BALI

    Upon arrival at Ngurah Rai International Airport (Denpasar International Airport), Meet and greet assistance by Tripgyani representative. Later you will be transferred to the hotel. Check-in to the hotel and relax. Enjoy rest of the time in leisure. Overnight stay in hotel in Bali.

  • DAY 02: ROMANTIC DINNER CRUISE

    After having breakfast, one can enjoy a refreshing spa treatment (own cost). In the afternoon you will be taken to the Benoa Harbor. Board on cruise to enjoy sunset while cruising. Watch traditional performances and dinner in the cruise. After cruise, return to the hotel for overnight stay.

  • DAY 03: KECAK - ULUWATU EXPERIENCE

    After having breakfast in the hotel, enjoy rest of the time in leisure. In the evening have a great Balinese sunset experience. You will be driven to Uluwatu. Visit the holy temple of Pura Uluwatu and then enjoy a Balinese Kecak dance performance. Enjoy a scrumptious dinner in night and return to the hotel for overnight stay.

  • DAY 04: BALI

    After having breakfast, entire day at leisure for shopping and other activities on own. Overnight stay at hotel in Bali.

  • DAY 05: DEPARTURE FROM BALI

    After Breakfast, check out of the hotel to proceed to the airport for your flight back home with wonderful memories.

SPLENDID BALI

Booknow

  • DAY 1 - ARRIVE BALI

    Upon arrival at Ngurah Rai International Airport (Denpasar International Airport), Meet and greet assistance by Tripgyani representative. Later you will be transferred to the hotel. Check-in to the hotel and relax. Enjoy rest of the time in leisure. Overnight stay in hotel in Bali.

  • DAY 2 - DAY AT LEISURE OR IF YOU HAVE AVAILED THE COMBO (FULL DAY KINTAMANI TOUR WITH UBUD) PROCEED FOR THE SAME

    After breakfast, a full day excursion to the Kintamani Volcano which will take you up close & personal with the essence of Bali. This is the most favorite tour of any first-time visitor to Bali. Visit the Batuan village (famous for traditional paintings), Mas village (famous for Wood carving) & then past the Celuk village (famous for silver and gold works). There after proceed to the Goa Gajah temple - Balinese ancient historical temple. Stop midway at the Kintamani Penelokan along the foot of Mount Batur with a splendid Batur lake view. A scrumptious Indonesian or an Indian lunch alongside this magical view will be a treat to all your senses alike (LUNCH NOT INCLUDED). Continue your tour to Tengalalang - a beautiful view of the rice terraces. Your tour ends in the famous Ubud Village. Here you can look in & around the various shopping options including the Ubud Art market. Overnight stay in hotel in Bali.

  • DAY 3 - DAY AT LEISURE OR IF YOU HAVE AVAILED THE COMBO (BALI SPA TREATMENT OR WATER SPORTS ACTIVITIES & SUNSET DINNER CRUISE) PROCEED FOR THE SAME

    After breakfast, proceed to Spa Treatment at Bali Orchids Spa or Similar or Either enjoy the water Sports Activities on Tanjung Benoa Beach and then transfer back to Hotel. Post lunch there’s no better way to spend a balmy tropical evening than on a beautiful evening Dinner Cruise. As the sun goes down you set off on a relaxing cruise around the harbour. Take in the cool night air on the top deck, be entertained by live musical entertainers at the bridge deck, savour a delicious international buffet dinner, then dance the night away at the disco or enjoy our live cabaret onboard. It is definitely a treat for all age groups especially the honeymooners. All in all a memorable evening at the Sunset cruise will always make you nostalgic in the future. Overnight stay in hotel in Bali.

  • DAY 4 - DAY AT LEISURE OR IF YOU HAVE AVAILED THE COMBO (ENJOY TANAH LOT TOUR) PROCEED FOR THE SAME

    After breakfast day at leisure, post lunch enjoy the Half Day Tour to Tanah Lot, this unique tour displays the vivid aspects of Balinese culture & it’s amazing flora & fauna. The tour starts with a visit to the Tanah Lot temple, built on top of large rock in the ocean, and famous as the sunset temple but fabulously beautiful in the morning as well. The amazing location of this place makes it a popular tourist spot. Overnight stay in hotel in Bali.

  • DAY 5 - DAY AT LEISURE OR CAN AVAIL THE OPTIONAL TOUR OF WHITE RIVER RAFTING OR LEMBONGAN CRUISE

    After Breakfast, day free at Leisure. Guest can do their own activity. Overnight stay in hotel in Bali.

  • DAY 6 - DEPARTURE BALI

    After Breakfast, check out of the hotel to proceed to the airport for your flight back home with wonderful memories.
