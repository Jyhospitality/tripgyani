Andaman Adventures

Booknow

Package starting from INR 18650/- Per person.For any customization in plan please call the Gyani’s.


  • DAY 1: ARRIVE PORT BLAIR

    Arrive Port Blair airport, meet and greet assistance by Tripgyani. And then transfer to the hotel. After check-in to the hotel take rest for few hours. In the evening visit to the Cellular Jail to experience the spectacular light and sound show. Stay overnight at Port Blair.

  • DAY 2: PORT BLAIR - CORAL ISLAND - PORT BLAIR

    After the breakfast enjoy a full-day tour to the North Bay or Coral Island. The place is good to view coral reefs, ornamental fishes and other water animals. A glass bottom boat ride is available to have a close look at the coral colonies. You can indulge in snorkeling, swimming and trekking too.

  • DAY 3: PORT BLAIR – HAVELOCK

    After breakfast transfer to port to visit beautiful island of Havelock. On arrival to Havelock you will be escorted to the Resort. Day is at leisure. Later visit the famous Radha Nagar beach, experience the beauty of blue waters. Stay Overnight at Havelock.

  • DAY 4: HAVELOCK - PORT BLAIR

    Enjoy an early breakfast at the hotel later as per ferry time, check out from the hotel and return back to Port Blair. Stay overnight at Port Blair.

  • DAY 5: PORT BLAIR

    Enjoy breakfast at the hotel later proceed for a full-day tour of places around Port Blair. City tour includes Forest Museum, Mini Zoo, Anthropological Museum, Fisheries (Marine) Museum, Centre and Naval Marine Museum (Samudrika). The Andaman Water Sports Complex is a good place to go for water-skiing, boating, water scooter rides, tube rides, etc. The Cottage Industries Emporium (Sagarika) is a good place to shop for curios. Stay overnight at Port Blair.

  • DAY 6: PORT BLAIR - ROSS ISLAND - PORT BLAIR

    Enjoy breakfast at the hotel later visit the famous Ross Island after this is where the British ruled the islands during the pre-independence era. The island abounds in deer and bird population and is ideal for taking leisure walks. Later take a trip to the Chidiyatapu or Bird Island (31kms from port Blair). Densely covered with mangroves, the island has imposing greenery and a beach that offers mesmerizing views of the sunset. Stay overnight at the hotel in Port Blair.

  • DAY 7: DEPARTURE PORT BLAIR

    Enjoy an early breakfast at the hotel, later drive to Port Blair airport for return journey to home with sweet memories of Andaman.
